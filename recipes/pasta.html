Pasta

This Italian dish is good for both lunch and dinner. Millions around the world enjoy it everyday so you should too!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. Boil 2 cups of pasta for 10-12 minutes
  3. Heat up sauce over medium low heat and serve to enjoy!
